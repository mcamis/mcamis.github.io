The Goods

Like Buzzfeed, but for bartenders

Design & Development

The Goods was Shiftgig's viral content site for service industry employees. I planned, designed and developed a responsive, full-stack platform within an existing Drupal codebase.

  • PHP
  • Drupal
  • HTML/CSS
  • jQuery
  • Analytics

Social & mobile-first

The intent behind The Goods was to grow via social sharing, so I knew that the majority of traffic would be coming from social networks, especially Facebook. With this in mind The Goods was optimized for sharing and reading from within Facebook's in-app browser.

Monetization and support

After setting up comprehensive audience targeting, analytics and Google DFP placements, I trained the marketing solutions team on how to execute ad campaigns with our tech. When posting articles, authors were able to choose placement and ranking as well as target specific verticals and job categories.
