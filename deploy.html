Deploy is a responsive web application used to manage Shiftgig's on-demand labor platform. The entire organization at Shiftgig — operations, HR, accounting, and sales — uses Deploy in their daily workflow.

  • Backbone.js
  • Javascript/ES6
  • HTML/CSS
  • Sketch

Notifications about notifications, updates about updates

In addition to a comprehensive UI stack, Deploy's interface gives users insight into how data is used across the entire product ecosystem. Simple interactions can trigger notifications, permission changes, dramatically change available content in customer applications and more. With this in mind everything has been designed to give informative, actionable feedback.
